Rooster

A Rooster is an adult male chicken. Roosters are larger, usually more brightly colored, and have larger combs on top of their heads compared to hens. A Rooster's crowing can be used to announce to neighboring fowl that a rooster is in residence, which is his territory and hens. Their hearing is so acute that they can determine where a noise comes from with almost pinpoint accuracy!
